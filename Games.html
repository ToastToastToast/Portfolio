The WildLife

The Wildlife is a p5.js game created inside the program Processing. This was my final project for Art 74. The story takes place in the snowy winter forest. A pack of wolves are doing there daily hunting routine. When you return from catching a rabbit, one of the pack is found dead! They all claim that it is you and the only option is to prove your innocence. Will you be falsely accused and executed for murder or will you find the true murderer?
