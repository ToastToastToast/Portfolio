"The Drunk Two", Digital Video, Linken Logs, plastic toys.

Using Linken Logs, I created an old western town with cowboys. A screen is projected in the bar near the back of
the room, giving the video an atmosphere of the wild west. The cowboys become drunk and do something
irrational to resemble becoming drunk can lead to terrible mistakes.
